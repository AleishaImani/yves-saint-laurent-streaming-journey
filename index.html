What is Streaming?

Streaming media is a multimedia that is delivered and consumed in a continuous manner from a source or in understandable words, streaming refers to a multimedia file such as audio and video that can be instantly played without the need to download it first. It works through a continuous transmission of the media content from a server to the client.

The Need Of Viewers

The needs of the viewers are catered to them in each streaming platforms whether it be a gaming content or a cooking content etc.

The need of the viewers is determined from what content type they are watching, sometimes they need responses and answers and sometimes they only need entertainment. The people who undertake the challenge of being a content creator need to learn a lot about what the viewers need.

Characteristics of a Good Streamer

- Being honest to the viewers, only telling or sharing facts to not lead into the spreading of misinformations
- Respecting your audience would help you create a healthy relationship with your viewers which will then make you gain more, it gives you a better image overall.
- Keep your stream entertaining to make the audience want to stay and watch your stream.
- Be aware of the words that are coming out of your mouth, think before you speak, and don’t be insensitive.

Characteristics of a Good Stream

- A fast paced chatbox, this indicates that the streamer is doing well in interacting with their audience
- A high viewer count, having a lot of people enjoy your content is a great sign you’re doing well in streaming.
- Having the stream go smoothly meant it was successful.
